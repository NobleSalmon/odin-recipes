Chocolate Cookies

Description

Ooey gooey chocolate chips made from scratch! Serve warm for best experience! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups flour (sift)
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 12-oz. pkg. chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 350°F.
  2. Beat butter, eggs, sugars, and vanilla until light and fluffy.
  3. Add dry ingredients; mix well until smooth.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Roll into balls, and evenly place onto ungreased cookie sheet.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Let sit for 5-10 mins on cooling rack. Serve, and enjoy!
