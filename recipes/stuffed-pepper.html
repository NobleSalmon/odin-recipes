Stuffed Peppers

Description

This is an easy healthy dish that requires little active cooking! Great for busy people who are trying to stay away from fast food. Low in fat and carbs, this healthy dish tastes amazing!

Ingredients

  • 6 large Bell peppers
  • 1 cup Brown rice
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 can (15 oz) Black beans
  • 1 lb 90/10 ground turkey
  • 4 oz. of shredded mozzarella
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Olive Olive
  • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to tastes
  • Optional: 1 cup of corn
  • Optional: Green chilis
  • Optional: Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Bring olive oil to a medium heat inside a large sauce pan.
  3. Bring water to a boil in a small to medium pot.
  4. Once oil is heated, carmelize the diced onion.
  5. After the onions are carmelized add in the minced garlic. Cook until fragrant.
  6. Add the turkey to the pot with onions and garlic, then cook until just browned.
  7. After adding the turkey, add rice to boiling water, reduce to a simmer, and cover. Let cook until fluffy and all water is gone, try to avoid stirring.
  8. Drain both crushed tomatoes and black beans, then add to the turkey. (also add corn and chilis here if you prefer them)
  9. Add spices to the turkey mix, and reduce heat.
  10. Once rice rice is cooked, add to turkey mix. Stir.
  11. Cut tops off bell peppers and remove seeds.
  12. Stuff peppers with rice and turkey mix, and top with shredded cheese.
  13. Array upright in a deep edged baking dish, and bake for 20-30 minutes or until cheese is browned.
  14. Garnish with cilantro (optional), serve and enjoy!
